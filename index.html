About MaRS

Thapar's one and only Robotics Society

Mechatronics and Robotics Society (MaRS) provides an ideal platform for students of Mechanical, Mechatronics, Electronics, Computers and Electrical Engineering background to hone their skills and showcase their talent in many interdisciplinary activities.

Events

YaY! Party is ONN

MaRS conducts different workshops (on Lego Kits, ROS, Arduino Uno, Raspberry Pi kits), competitions, technical quizzes and expert lectures on a regular basis.
